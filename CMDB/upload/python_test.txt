列表推导排序是key的用法:
>>> a = [[1,'d'],[8,'c'],[20,'c']]
>>> sorted(a ,key=lambda a:a[0])
[[1, 'd'], [8, 'c'], [20, 'c']]
#lambda函数自动定义里面的项。
>>> students = [('john','A',15),('jane','b',12),('dave','B',10)]
>>> sorted(students,key=lambda student:student[2])
[('dave', 'B', 10), ('jane', 'b', 12), ('john', 'A', 15)]
